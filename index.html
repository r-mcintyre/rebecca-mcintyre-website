Hi, I'm Rebecca!

I think therapy is a strange and beautiful thing, and I love helping people navigate its weirdness.

People come to me when they want something to change. Maybe they don’t know what that is, exactly, but I don’t think they should have to figure that out alone. What can therapy do for you?

Let’s find out together; schedule a free 15-minute consultation through my booking page, or click below to learn more about me.

About me

Services

I provide individual online therapy to adults (18+) across Ontario. I welcome clients of all backgrounds. I work most often with:

  • Depression
  • Anxiety
  • Stress
  • Self-esteem
  • Perfectionism
  • Neurodiversity
  • Learning disabilities
  • Executive functioning
  • Questioning gender and/or sexuality
  • Spiritual or philosophical concerns
